## Expected Analysis Outcomes

1. **Account maturity**: Under 90 days — must use leading indicators (Contact Rate, Quote Rate), NOT Sold CPA for health assessment.
2. **Leading indicators are strong**: Contact Rate 70% (>50%) + Quote Rate 50% (High). Interpretation: "Leads are strong. Agency is engaging. CPA will mature favorably. Hold current settings."
3. **Missing RightPricing**: Must be flagged as a critical gap. No specific bid recommendations should be finalized without it.
4. **Do NOT recommend shutting down**: The data shows a healthy account maturing normally. The $890 CPA the client calculated is misleading because disposition data takes time to flow through.
5. **Partial month projection**: March should be projected (18/25 days × 31 = ~22 projected leads).
6. **Geo-buffer note**: Client targeting only DFW metro — if/when RightPricing is obtained, a 20-30% geo-buffer should be applied.
