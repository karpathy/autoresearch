## Expected Analysis Outcomes

1. **Root Cause**: Competitive Positioning (searches stable/up, win rate down, impression share down)
2. **QuoteWizard**: Win rate problem. Effective bid = $18.50 × 1.04 = $19.24, sitting between 2nd and 3rd RPC. Recommend raising multiplier toward 1st position.
3. **MediaAlpha**: Bid rate problem (2.1%). Do NOT recommend raising bid. Investigate targeting/filters.
4. **EverQuote**: Performing well (33.7% win rate). Effective bid $17.58, above 3rd RPC. Hold steady.
5. **SmartFinancial**: Win rate problem (12.4%). Effective bid = $18.50. Between 2nd and 3rd RPC. Recommend raising multiplier.
6. **InsuranceLeads**: Performing well (30.2%). Effective bid = $21.28, above 1st RPC. Hold steady or consider reducing.
7. **Bid lever**: Should use multiplier adjustments (not base bid) because only specific sources have problems while others perform well.
8. **Client constraint**: "a little" means incremental, not aggressive. Should not recommend 2× strategy.
