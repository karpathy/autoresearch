# Project EcoTrack

## Vision
A mobile app that helps individuals track their carbon footprint in real-time by integrating with their bank accounts and transportation apps.

## Goals
1. Provide accurate, automated carbon tracking.
2. Offer personalized suggestions for reducing footprint.
3. Gamify the experience to encourage long-term engagement.

## Target Audience
Environmentally conscious millennials and Gen Z who want to take actionable steps but find manual tracking too tedious.
